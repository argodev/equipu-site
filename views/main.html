We at Kids 4 Truth are glad you're here! EquipU is our online library of free children's ministry resources, put together for parents, church leaders, youth workers, and others working with children worldwide. EquipU is a massive compendium of relevant, fine-tuned materials designed to help you teach children God-focused truth in a God-focused way.

Since it is web-based, EquipU facilitates efficient support and community discussion. It is a place where workers can find materials and interact with one another to share tips and tools that have improved their own ministries.

GET STARTED

Again, EquipU is free. The following items are a few highlights of EquipU's features:

TRUTHTIME TEACHER LESSON PLANS

We are constantly developing new and updated teacher lesson plans. Inside EquipU, you will find a document library that contains the most up-to-date collection of lesson plans and other official, clubs-specific, staff-designed teacher resources.

USER CONTRIBUTIONS

Many people use the Kids 4 Truth curriculum in different ways to meet their own various ministry needs. Different approaches to the same "problem" may work better in different situations. On EquipU, you will find materials that have been created and contributed by other children's ministry workers who want to share what they have found helpful in their environment. These resources include things such as attendance sheets, training resources, ideas for activities, and more.

MUCH MORE!

We are constantly posting improvements and updating the resources on EquipU. Streaming video and audio training sessions from the Kids 4 Truth staff, surveys and results, feedback forms, etc. are all available here on EquipU. We would love to have you learning right along with us!
